Welcome to Phoebus!

Phoebus is the software that drives Add-ons Sites targeting many UXP Applications including Pale Moon, Basilisk, and more.

What is the Panel?

The Phoebus Panel allows Add-on Developers to manage their extensions and themes contained on an Add-ons Site. You can submit and update Add-ons that supports any of the Applications that Phoebus currently serves.

How do I get started?

On the banner at the top right you can select Your Add-ons to manage your extensions and themes. Additionally, you can select Your Account to alter your account settings such as your display name and password.

Important Information

Firefox and Thunderbird ID em:targetApplication blocks in Pale Moon Extensions

Many Pale Moon Extensions still retain their Firefox and Thunderbird em:targetApplication entries from the fantastic forking efforts. However, now that Basilisk and Interlink exist and Phoebus is multi-application aware, this is a problem because Basilisk uses Firefox's ID and Interlink uses Thunderbird's and will display any add-on that claims support in their install manifest file.

Thus, we ask you to re-evaluate your extensions for Basilisk and Interlink compatiblity and if support is not desired to remove the relevant em:targetApplication blocks from the install manifest as soon as possible.

This will help to avoid unwanted issue and support requests from those, understandably, trying to run your extensions in Basilisk or Interlink.
